# Support and SLA

## MVP support
Founder-led. Email/Slack/Teams shared channel for pilots. No formal SLA until enterprise readiness.

## Pilot support
- Kickoff.
- Setup support.
- Findings review.
- Report walkthrough.
- Remediation review.

## Later severity levels
- Sev1: customer data/security incident.
- Sev2: assessment blocked.
- Sev3: noncritical product issue.
- Sev4: question/feature request.

Do not promise enterprise SLA before operational readiness.
