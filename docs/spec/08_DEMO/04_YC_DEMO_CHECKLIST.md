# YC / Customer Demo Checklist

## Before demo
- Reset workspace.
- Seed data.
- Run scenarios.
- Confirm findings.
- Export report.
- Prepare backup screenshots/video.
- Check Azure spend and service health.

## Must show
- Pain before product.
- One clear exposure path.
- Evidence.
- Remediation.
- Report.
- Why buyer pays.

## Must not show
- Long setup.
- Debug screens.
- Generic charts.
- AI magic claims.
- Broad roadmap too early.

## Strong signals
- "We have this."
- "Can you test our tenant?"
- "How much is assessment?"
- "Can you send report?"

## Weak signals
- "Interesting."
- "Keep us updated."
- "Maybe later."
