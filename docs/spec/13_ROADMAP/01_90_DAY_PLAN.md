# 90-Day Plan

## Month 1 - Validate and demo
Product: demo data, rule engine, dashboard, report, Graph spike.
Customer: 30 interviews, 100 prospects, 5 demos, 3 pricing conversations.
Decision: continue only if buyer urgency and paid pilot intent are real.

## Month 2 - Paid pilot readiness
Product: metadata-only customer scan, scope-limited Graph, remediation tracker, security packet, customer-cloud draft.
Customer: close 1-2 design partners, run first controlled assessment.

## Month 3 - Convert learning into product
Product: re-scan/proof-of-fix, false-positive workflow, agent inventory spike, exports.
Customer: convert pilot to annual/second scope, get referenceable learning, test partners/MSPs.

## Weekly milestones
1. Demo skeleton and 8 calls booked.
2. Graph spike and 10 interviews.
3. Report and 5 demos.
4. Security packet and pricing asks.
5. Customer scan and pilot scoping.
6. Findings review and close pilot.
7. Remediation tracker and pilot delivery.
8. Re-scan and pilot report.
9. Agent inventory spike.
10. Customer-cloud draft.
11. Reliability and references.
12. V1 packaging and continue/raise/pivot.
