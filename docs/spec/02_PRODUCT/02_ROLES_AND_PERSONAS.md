# Roles and Personas

## CISO - buyer
Wants confidence before rollout, risk reduction, board-ready proof, and a clear reason to buy beyond Microsoft tooling.

Objection: "We already pay for Microsoft E5."
Answer: "Use Microsoft controls. We show which exposure paths to fix and prove whether remediation worked."

## Security architect - daily user
Wants scenario evidence, low false positives, and repeatable reports.

Objection: "I do not need another dashboard."
Answer: "The dashboard is just the workflow. The product delivers exposure paths and remediation."

## M365 admin - technical user
Wants permission evidence, file/site owners, safe scope, and minimal disruption.

Objection: "I do not want to grant broad tenant access."
Answer: "Start sandbox, metadata-only, scope-limited, or customer-cloud."

## AI governance lead - champion
Wants to unblock Copilot rollout and produce evidence for security committees.

Objection: "This might become consulting."
Answer: "The assessment is software-driven and repeatable. Services are only onboarding."
