# Design Partner Offer

## Offer
Two-week Copilot Exposure Assessment.

## Promise
Run a controlled metadata-first assessment for a scoped Microsoft 365 environment and deliver top exposure paths plus Microsoft-native remediation steps.

## Options
- Demo-only review: no customer access.
- Metadata-only assessment: narrow approved scope.
- Customer-cloud pilot: scanner runs in customer Azure.

## Deliverables
- Scope document.
- Findings dashboard.
- Top 20 exposure findings.
- Evidence chains.
- Remediation plan.
- Executive report.
- Optional proof-of-fix retest.

## Pricing test
- Early design partner: $5k-$10k if they give fast feedback/reference.
- Standard pilot: $15k.
- Enterprise/customer-cloud pilot: $25k+.

## Ask
"If we scoped this to one SharePoint area and produced a report in two weeks, who would approve a $10k pilot?"
