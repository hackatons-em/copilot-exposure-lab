# Sales Process

## Stage 1 - Problem call
Learn pain, tools, buyer, blocker. Do not pitch too early.

## Stage 2 - Demo
Show exposure path, evidence, remediation, report.

## Stage 3 - Scope
Define tenant/site/departments, metadata-only/content sampling, customer-cloud need, reviewer.

## Stage 4 - Paid pilot ask
Offer 2-4 week assessment at $10k-$25k.

## Stage 5 - Pilot delivery
Scan, findings review, report, remediation plan, optional re-scan.

## Stage 6 - Conversion
Annual platform, continuous monitoring, more scopes.

## Rule
Do not sell "AI security." Sell "unblock Copilot rollout with exposure evidence and remediation."
