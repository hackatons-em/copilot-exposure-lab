# Design Principles

## Feel
Calm, sharp, enterprise-trustworthy, Microsoft-native, not noisy.

## Rules
- Evidence before opinion.
- Remediation before charts.
- Every number clickable to evidence.
- No fake AI magic.
- No decorative complexity.
- Clean tables and strong hierarchy.
- Minimal animation.
- Security buyer language.

## Product hierarchy
1. What is exposed?
2. Why?
3. Who can reach it?
4. What scenario found it?
5. What should be fixed?
6. How do we prove it is fixed?
