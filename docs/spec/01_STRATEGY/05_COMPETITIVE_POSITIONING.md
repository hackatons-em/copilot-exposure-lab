# Competitive Positioning

## Positioning
Not: "We scan SharePoint."
Yes: "We simulate Copilot and agent exposure paths, then produce evidence and remediation before rollout."

## Alternatives

| Alternative | Strength | Weakness | Our angle |
|---|---|---|---|
| Microsoft Purview | Native governance and compliance | Not always packaged as exposure drill and proof-of-fix workflow | Use it as remediation destination |
| Microsoft Defender | Security monitoring | More runtime/security events than pre-rollout business report | Scenario-based pre-rollout proof |
| SharePoint Advanced Management | Oversharing controls | Narrower than AI exposure scenario workflow | Map findings to SAM controls |
| Data security vendors | Broad DSPM | May be expensive/broad and not Copilot-drill focused | Win with focus and speed |
| Consultants | Can review manually | Slow, non-repeatable | Productized assessment |
| Internal scripts | Cheap | Weak reporting, brittle, no benchmark | 10x faster evidence/reporting |

## Wedge statement
An independent exposure lab that helps teams prove what can be exposed before expanding Copilot.
