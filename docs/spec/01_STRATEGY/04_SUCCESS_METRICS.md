# Success Metrics

## 30-day validation metrics
- 30 qualified interviews.
- 10+ confirm active Copilot/M365 AI exposure concern.
- 5+ ask for demo or sample report.
- 3+ discuss paid assessment pricing.
- 1+ design partner next step.

## MVP demo metrics
- Demo under 5 minutes.
- At least 5 realistic findings.
- Every critical finding has evidence and remediation.
- Report exports.
- Buyer understands value without explanation overload.

## Product metrics
- Time to first finding under 15 minutes in demo.
- Time to report under 30 minutes in demo.
- Every finding has a traceable evidence chain.
- Re-scan can prove a fix.

## Kill metrics
- 5+ qualified buyers say Microsoft already solves it well enough.
- No buyer can name budget owner.
- No buyer wants an assessment.
- Trust barrier blocks even metadata-only/customer-cloud pilots.
