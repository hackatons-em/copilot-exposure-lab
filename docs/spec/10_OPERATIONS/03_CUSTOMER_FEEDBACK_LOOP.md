# Customer Feedback Loop

## Classify feedback
- Pain confirmation.
- Pain rejection.
- Workflow detail.
- Buyer detail.
- Budget detail.
- Trust blocker.
- Feature request.
- Competitive objection.
- Demo confusion.
- Pricing reaction.

## CRM fields
Company, role, industry, Copilot status, current tools, pain score, urgency score, budget clarity, trust blocker, next step, quote, decision signal.

## Rules
- One quote is not validation.
- Repeated patterns matter.
- Feature requests from non-buyers are low priority.
- Pricing reactions matter more than compliments.
